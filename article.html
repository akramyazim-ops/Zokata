The Ultimate Guide to WhatsApp Business API

Discover how transitioning from the standard WhatsApp Business app to the official API can 10x your sales team's productivity.

Why Upgrade to the API?

The standard WhatsApp Business application is great for solo entrepreneurs. However, as your team grows, sharing a single phone becomes a bottleneck. The Official WhatsApp Business API solves this by allowing multiple agents to log into the same number simultaneously using a CRM like Zokata.

1. Multi-Agent Support

With the API, you can have your entire sales and support team handling inquiries from one central WhatsApp number. No more lost leads, delayed responses, or phones dying during a critical product launch.

lightbulb Quick Tip

Zokata automatically routes new leads to available agents, ensuring a response time of under 5 minutes.

2. Advanced Automation

Set up intelligent auto-replies based on keywords or business hours. Guide prospects through a qualification flow before a human agent even steps in. Imagine waking up to pre-qualified leads ready to buy.

3. Rich Messaging & Templates

Send interactive buttons, product lists, and rich media directly in the chat, creating a mini-app experience right within WhatsApp. Furthermore, use pre-approved templates to initiate conversations or send vital updates notifications safely.
